Branding Ideas

What does the company do, or offer?

The company KOSMOS functions as a online shopping platform that offers K-Pop merchandise ranging from hats, t-shirts, accessories, and more! Its overall aesthetics is cute and youthful with a hint of retro vibes, these are to target the younger audiences.

Who is the company to potential customers? (as a person)

They are a trusted shopping source that will provide a smooth and enjoyable shopping environment for the customers.

Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service

With its main target audience being K-pop enjoyers who enjoys the bright and youthful aesthetic, this will entice them to buy the merchandise and more.
